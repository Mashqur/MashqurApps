About

iOS craft with product judgment.

Swift-first Performance-minded Release-ready

I am Mashqur Habib Himel, a Senior iOS Developer with 9+ years of experience building native mobile applications and 14+ years of overall software engineering experience. My work spans greenfield builds, legacy modernization, performance tuning, real-time communication, and App Store launches.

My day-to-day toolkit includes Swift, SwiftUI, UIKit, Objective-C, Combine, Core Data, RESTful APIs, and pragmatic architecture patterns like MVVM, MVC, and Clean Architecture. I care about code that is readable, testable, and friendly to the next person who has to ship with it.

I am especially passionate about clean interaction design, smooth app performance, thoughtful state management, and building engineering habits that help teams move quickly without accumulating avoidable complexity.
